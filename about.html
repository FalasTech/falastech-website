ABOUT / THE INITIATIVE

FalasTech is an independent initiative focused on building simple, privacy-respecting web tools for public use and digital advocacy. The project is independently maintained and aims to create tools that are accessible, transparent, and easy to use. FalasTech may expand to include collaborators in the future.

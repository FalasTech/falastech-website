Welcome to the
FalasTech Launchpad

The FalasTech Launchpad — it's a vibrant community rooted in allyship, solidarity, and a shared mission for justice. We use activism to empower impactful projects focused on Palestinian advocacy and liberation.

We take on a select number of projects. We're also committed to spotlighting other community-driven initiatives, ensuring that important efforts receive the visibility and support they deserve.

Our mission is simple: to build tools that matter, foster digital resistance, and create sustainable impact through technology.

Get Involved Join Discord Explore Projects

What You’ll Gain:

  • Join a supportive community of like-minded individuals.
  • Access to a dynamic community space for collaboration and resource-sharing.
  • Visibility for your projects through our platform, with opportunities for media promotion and exposure.
  • Ability to brand as FalasTech in certain cases like using a FalasTech email or logo.

What We Expect:

  • Commitment to projects with high impact, aiming to create meaningful change.
  • Engagement with the community through regular updates or participation in discussions.
  • Understand that we may remove projects if they’re not a fit, without hard feelings.

What We Don’t Require:

  • Daily meetings/discussions.
  • Mandatory use of our infrastructure.
  • Official or FalasTech-branded project.

To Join:

  • When you're ready with a project — Apply to join the Launchpad
